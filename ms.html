How to Play Minesweeper

Minesweeper is a classic puzzle game. The goal is to clear a rectangular board containing hidden "mines" without detonating any of them, with help from clues about the number of neighboring mines in each field.

Instructions:

  • Left Click a tile to reveal what is underneath it.
  • Right Click (or hold, on mobile devices) to place flags on tiles you suspect contain bombs.
  • The numbers on the board represent how many bombs are adjacent to a square. For example, if a square has a "3" on it, that means there are 3 bombs adjacent to that square.
  • Use these clues to safely clear all the non-mined squares.
