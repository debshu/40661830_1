Mahjong: The Classic Tile Game

How to Play Mahjong

Mahjong is a tile-based game developed in China during the Qing dynasty. It is commonly played by four players, with some variations in different regions. The game is known for its beautiful, hand-carved tiles and complex strategic gameplay.

Objective: The goal is to build sets and get the highest point value using a combination of drawn and discarded tiles to form four melds and a pair.
