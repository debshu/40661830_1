Minesweeper

How to Play

Minesweeper is a classic puzzle game. The goal is to clear a board containing hidden mines without detonating any, with help from clues about the number of neighboring mines in each field.

  • Left Click a tile to reveal what is underneath.
  • Right Click to place flags on suspected mines.
  • Numbers show how many mines are adjacent to that square.
  • Clear all non-mined squares to win the game.
